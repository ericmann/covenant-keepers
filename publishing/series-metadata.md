## Series Description

When twelve-year-old Eli and his ten-year-old sister Elena discover a mysterious bronze device hidden in their grandfather's study, they are pulled backward through time into the events of the Bible. Joined by their skeptical neighbor Jordan, the three kids must find twelve ancient relics -- one from each moment where God's faithfulness was on display -- before a creeping force called the Fade can erase those moments from memory forever.

From the Valley of Elah to the deck of Noah's Ark, from the furnace of Babylon to the summit of Mount Moriah, the Covenant Keepers series takes young readers on twelve adventures across sacred history. Each book immerses kids in a Biblical narrative they thought they knew and shows them what it feels like to stand in the dust, hear the voices, and witness the promises firsthand. Along the way, Eli learns to trust without needing all the answers, Elena discovers that courage takes many forms, and Jordan begins an honest journey from skepticism toward something he cannot quite name.

With the pacing of a time-travel adventure, the heart of a family story, and the depth of Scripture brought to life, Covenant Keepers is a series for kids who love action, mystery, and stories that stay with them long after the last page.
