## About the Series

**Covenant Keepers** is a twelve-book adventure series for readers ages 8-12. When Eli, Elena, and Jordan discover a mysterious bronze device called the Pathfinder in their grandfather's hidden study, they are pulled backward through time into the events of the Bible. Their mission: find twelve ancient relics before a creeping force called the Fade can erase God's promises from memory. Each book is a self-contained adventure set within an ongoing story of faith, friendship, and discovery.
